You can make multiple copies of any sheet and change one cell to a list of values.

 

You can do this in one of two ways:

1. Putting a list in the 'Data Validation' of the cell you want to change.

2. Making a separate list and using the 'Make Bulk List' button.

 

If you want to delete the sheets you made, you can use 'undo' (Ctrl-Z) or use the 'Delete Bulk Sheets' button.
